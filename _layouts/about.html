About Us

Nile Global Trading was born in the Chinese coastal city of Ningbo, home to one of the world's largest shipping ports.

We are a team of African, Middle Eastern and Chinese business professionals with a common purpose of simplifying international trade for Middle Eastern and African businesses.

Our Mission

We believe that countries across the One Belt One Road Initiative have a great potential to grow their business with China, yet currently the greatest beneficiaries are mostly Chinese companies. Our goal is to make trading with China more accessible and profitable for your business.

To be the number one global supply chain company

facilitating trade between China, Middle East and Africa.
